About Me

I am Louis Carstens, a passionate game developer and artist who originates from South Africa.
I am looking to create and share the worlds and characters within my imagination so that other people can experience them.

Currently, I primarily work on Unity, but I am exploring the possibility of transitioning to Unreal 5 and C++.
When it comes to programming I have an interest in designing and creating AI systems for characters and enemies and finding unique ways the players can interact with them.

Additionally I am learning how to use blender to create 3D models and eventually how to create animations.

Specific things about me:
I am a minimalist and enjoy having small yet effective designs and I am constantly looking into how to optimize my workflow.
